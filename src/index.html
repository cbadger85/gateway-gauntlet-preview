The Gateway Gauntlet

Greetings! We are proud to announce the second Gateway Gauntlet! It will be held on the 29th and 30th of June at Game Nite in St. Louis, MO. The number of entrants will be 64 and the entry fee will be $35. All participants will receive an event patch! This is a two day, 300 point, ITS event. No add-ons are being used.

Gauntlet Challenge

The Gauntlet Challenge is an event exclusive to the Gateway Gauntlet. At the beginning of the third round, each table will have a challenge patch placed on it. The winner of the third round will receive the patch. The receipent will then display the patch prominently on their bag, case, or any other velcro'd surface.
